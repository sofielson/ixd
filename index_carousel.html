Intro

We all look different, that is a fact. Should our appearance really affect how much we earn, what kind of jobs we get and how we are treated in the workplace?

We strongly believe that one of the things that makes you you, is the unique mix we all are of different people, gender, race, personality and appearances.

However, people are still being discriminated against in the workplace due to the colour of their skin, gender, tattoos, piercings, hair color, scars etc. As Martin Luther King Jr said in 1963:

"I have a dream that my four children will one day live in a nation where they will not be judged by the colour of their skin, but by the content of their character”

Still, 60 years later here we are… People are still being judged for everything except the content of their character: colour of their skin, gender, nationality, sexual orientation - you name it!

With this project, we hope to highlight the struggles everyone goes through on a day-to-day basis, and evoke empathy for the minorities in the workplace. We hope that visitors will leave the installation feeling a bit better informed and ready to become the change we need for a more diverse, equal and inclusive workplace.
